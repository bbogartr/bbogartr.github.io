bio


My name's Ben Rickles. I'm from New Hope, Pennsylvania. I received my undergraduate degree from the University of Pittsburgh in 2009, a master's degree from the University of Oregon in 2017, and my PhD in 2023 in Neuroscience and Cognitive Science at the University of Maryland.

My current research uses electroencephalography (EEG) to probe the biological mechanisms underlying narrative comprehension. My other research interests include the psychological phenomena surrounding word learning from context.

My hope is to apply my knowledge to improve neurotech in the healthcare industry.

My hobbies include cooking elaborate meals with my amazing partner, watching movies, tinkering with electronics, and riding my bike around Jupiter, Florida.

I'm currently looking for a job so let me know if you're hiring!
