Narrative Comprehension

Story Grammars

If you are reading a story, how do you know whether this part is important or not? The answer to the question lies in how you represent the story in memory. What does the brain use to do this? My lab is putting together study to help find out. We are going to be using a variety of techniques to quantify the imortance of each part of the story. We then plan to look at brain processes using EEG, recorded while participants listen to a variety of folktales (5-8 minutes in length).
